Liability & Legal Expenses (Ansvar/Rättsskydd)​

Liability coverage (ansvarsskydd) handles damage the policyholder accidentally causes to others, while legal expenses coverage (rättsskydd) covers legal costs in qualifying civil disputes. These user stories cover the complete liability and legal expenses claim lifecycle including liability assessment, settlement negotiation, defense against unfounded claims, rättsskydd eligibility, lawyer approval, and BRF vs individual liability boundaries.
