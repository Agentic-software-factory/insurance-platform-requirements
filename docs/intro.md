# Introduction

Business requirements documentation for **TryggFörsäkring AB**, a mid-size Swedish insurance company replacing a 15+ year old legacy policy administration system.

## Purpose

This documentation site captures the formalized business requirements needed by internal development teams to build the replacement platform. It provides:

- **Actors and Personas** — who interacts with the system
- **User Stories and Use Cases** — what the system must do
- **Regulatory Traceability** — FSA, GDPR, and IDD compliance mappings

## Phased Delivery

| Phase                         | Scope                                                                          |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Phase 1 — Motor Insurance** | Personal motor policies, claims handling, underwriting rules, trafikförsäkring |
| **Phase 2 — Home & Property** | Homeowner policies, property damage claims, natural disaster coverage          |
| **Phase 3 — Commercial**      | Business policies, liability coverage, fleet insurance, commercial property    |

## Audience

- **Business analysts and product owners** — primary authors
- **Internal development teams** — primary consumers
- **Compliance and legal team** — reviewers
- **Underwriters and claims handlers** — domain experts, reviewers
